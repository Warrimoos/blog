I'm Steve, founder of [Warrimoo](https://warrimoo.net) — a small software studio on the Mid North Coast of Australia, building apps for pet owners and veterinary professionals.

This blog is where I write about the things I'm curious about: artificial intelligence, building software, veterinary technology, and whatever else catches my attention.

## Why "Warrimoo"?

Warrimoo is an Aboriginal word meaning "eagles nearby" — a nod to the place that shaped who I am. Like the eagle, we aim to see the bigger picture while staying grounded in what matters.

## What you'll find here

- **AI & Technology** — exploring how artificial intelligence is changing the way we build software and live our lives
- **Veterinary Tech** — insights from building [TreatMyPet](https://warrimoo.net/apps/treatmypet.html) and thinking about how technology can help pet owners
- **Building Things** — lessons learned from shipping software as a small team
- **Whatever else** — sometimes you just need to write about something
